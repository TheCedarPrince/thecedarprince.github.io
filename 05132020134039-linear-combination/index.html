How Does Linear Combination Operate?

Let vvv and www be any two vectors and let ccc and ddd represent any real numbers. Multiplying vvv and www by ccc and ddd respectively yields cvcvcv and dwdwdw.

Adding cvcvcv and dwdwdw together is the idea of a linear combination.
